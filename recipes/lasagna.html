Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

Use these ingredients to make a delicious Lasagna:

  • 1LBS ground beef
  • 1 Onion Chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook and stir ground beef, onion, and garlic until well browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, diced tomatoes, and water.
  3. Season with sugar, basil, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Simmer, uncovered, for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
