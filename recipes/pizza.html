Pizza Recipe

Homemade Pizza

Follow this recipe to make a delicious homemade pizza!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 3/4 cup of warm water
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it has doubled in size.
